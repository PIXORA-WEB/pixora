About PIXORA

From websites to a product-focused app studio.

Product-led direction

PIXORA is evolving from a web development service into a product-led digital brand, focused on practical apps that are useful, polished and easy to use.

The main direction is now PIXORA apps — especially finance tools and creative digital experiences that remove clutter and keep the essentials clear.

Website work is still available for selected projects, but it now sits behind the app-first PIXORA brand.

Finance credibility

PIXORA Track and PIXORA Budget are shaped by real finance experience, with a focus on clarity, practical dashboards and better money habits.

Product priorities

  • No bloated features
  • Clean app interfaces
  • Helpful everyday tools
  • Beta feedback before launch
