About PIXORA

Modern web development focused on clean code, performance and clarity.

PIXORA builds modern websites using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, with a focus on speed, responsive design and SEO-friendly structure. Every project is built cleanly, carefully structured and designed to support real business goals.

Alongside web development services, PIXORA is also developing digital apps under the brand, including PIXORA Finance Lite and PIXORA Play.

Based in La Línea de la Concepción, supporting English-speaking businesses across Spain and remotely.
