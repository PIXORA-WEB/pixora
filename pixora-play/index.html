PIXORA Play

PIXORA Play is a children’s app in development, designed to offer simple, creative and enjoyable digital activities. The initial launch is planned to focus on colouring, with future updates intended to expand the experience over time.

What it is

PIXORA Play is being developed as a child-friendly app under the PIXORA brand, focused on creativity, enjoyment and accessible digital interaction. The aim is to create a playful experience that feels simple to use while leaving room for new ideas and features as the app evolves.

Initial launch focus

The first version of PIXORA Play is planned as a kids colouring app, giving children a fun and straightforward way to enjoy creative screen time through digital colouring activities.

Creative direction

PIXORA Play is intended to focus on a more imaginative and playful product experience, with an emphasis on creativity, friendly design and simple interaction suitable for younger users.

Planned future features

Over time, PIXORA Play is expected to expand beyond colouring to include additional features such as doodling, puzzles and other interactive activities designed to make the app more varied and engaging.

Long-term vision

The long-term goal for PIXORA Play is to build a broader children’s app experience that can grow over time with new creative tools, playful ideas and additional features, while keeping the product easy to understand and enjoyable to use.

Approach

PIXORA Play is being planned with a focus on simplicity, accessibility and a positive user experience, aiming to create a digital environment that feels approachable, engaging and suitable for children.
