What it is

PIXORA Play is being developed as a child-friendly colouring app under the PIXORA brand, focused on creativity, enjoyment and accessible digital interaction.

The aim is to create a playful experience that feels simple to use, with a clean layout and a calm creative experience for children.

Launch focus

The first version of PIXORA Play is focused on colouring, giving children a simple and enjoyable way to engage with creative screen time without unnecessary complexity.

Pricing

PIXORA Play will be available as a free app with ads. There will also be an optional premium version at €1.99 per month or €11.99 per year.

At this stage, premium is best seen as a way to remove ads and support continued development of the PIXORA Play colouring experience.

Future ideas

Over time, PIXORA Play may expand beyond colouring with additional creative activities, while keeping the core experience simple and child-friendly.

Long-term vision

The long-term goal is to build a children’s creative app that can grow carefully over time, while keeping the product easy to understand and enjoyable to use.

Approach

PIXORA Play is being planned with a focus on simplicity, accessibility and a positive user experience, aiming to create a digital environment that feels approachable, engaging and suitable for children.
