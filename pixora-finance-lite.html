PIXORA Finance Lite

PIXORA Finance Lite is a finance app in development for self-employed professionals and freelancers who want a simpler way to record transactions, monitor cash position and review essential financial information.

What it is

PIXORA Finance Lite is being designed as a practical finance app for users who want a clear overview of business activity without the unnecessary complexity of larger accounting platforms. The aim is to provide a straightforward way to manage key figures, track transactions and stay on top of cash more confidently.

Dashboard

The dashboard is designed to give users a quick view of the most important figures, including monthly revenue, expenses and net profit, as well as year-to-date revenue, expenses and net profit.

It will also show current cash, amounts due to be paid out and available cash, supported by graphs to make financial performance easier to understand at a glance.

Transactions Paid

Users will be able to add paid transactions and view recorded entries in one place, helping maintain a clear history of completed business activity.

Transactions Unpaid

Users will be able to add future or unpaid transactions, then either delete them or mark them as paid when they are settled.

Cash Overview

The cash section will show current cash, amounts due to be paid out and available cash. Available cash is intended to reflect cash after liabilities, such as VAT.

This section will also display bank accounts added into the app and their balances.

Profit & Loss

Users will be able to view a profit and loss summary for the current month or on a year-to-date basis, providing a clearer picture of business performance over time.

Balance Sheet

A simplified balance sheet view will provide a basic breakdown of key balances in a clear and accessible format.

Manage

The manage section will allow users to maintain bank accounts added into the app and manage any custom categories they create to support their own workflow.

Settings

Settings will include options such as changing currency, setting the financial year start, adding categories, adding banks, exporting data to CSV, closing the financial year, exporting financial year data and creating backups.

Privacy and data storage

PIXORA Finance Lite is intended to store user-entered data locally rather than on PIXORA-managed servers. PIXORA does not hold or store the financial information entered into the app.

Because the data is stored locally, users will be responsible for backing up and storing their own data securely on their computer, iCloud, Google Drive or another trusted storage solution.
