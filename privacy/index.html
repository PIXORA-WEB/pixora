2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use this website:

  • Name

  • Budget or project information (if provided)

3. How We Collect Data

We collect data when:

  • You submit a contact form enquiry
  • You communicate with us by email

Contact form submissions are processed via Formspree, which securely transmits your enquiry data to our email address.

4. PIXORA Mobile Applications

PIXORA may publish mobile applications, including but not limited to:

  • PIXORA Play – a creative colouring and activity app
  • PIXORA Track – a personal finance utility app

These mobile applications may operate independently from the PIXORA website.


Data Collection in Mobile Applications

At the time of publication, PIXORA mobile applications:

  • do not require user accounts
  • do not collect personally identifiable information from users
  • do not require users to submit personal information in order to use core app functionality

Local Device Data

Some app data may be stored locally on the user’s device for functionality purposes only. This data is not transmitted to PIXORA servers unless explicitly stated in a future version of the application.


Future Features

Future versions of PIXORA mobile applications may include optional features such as:

  • advertising
  • in-app purchases
  • subscriptions
  • analytics
  • cloud-based features or account functionality

If such features are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before or at the time those features become available.


Children’s Privacy

PIXORA applications may be used by a general audience, including children in some cases. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children through our mobile applications unless clearly stated and lawfully permitted.


Contact for App Privacy Questions

For questions regarding PIXORA mobile applications, contact: hello@pixora.es
